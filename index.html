A pedometer was implemented using accelerometer values to track the user's steps and a compass was implemented to determine the direction of travel. In order to give the user directions, a simple path finding algorithm was implemented.

Nanoleaf

Nanoleaf is an innovative green lighting company founded by 3 University of Toronto graduates currently boasting the world's most energy efficient light bulb.


At Nanoleaf, my main role was Product and Software Testing. We used Agile software development practices; partipating in daily scrums and stand-ups. I conducted and wrote in-house test cases for the Nanoleaf Smarter Kit (Smart Light Bulb) and the Nanoleaf Aurora (Smart LED Light Panels). Another portion of my work was working and developing in an OpenWRT system. I also participated in app design meetings.


On top of the day to day duties at Nanoleaf, I provided tech support to customers through email and over the phone. I also took and edited promotional videos/photos for the website and potential buyers.

And I sold the Mayor of Toronto a light bulb!
